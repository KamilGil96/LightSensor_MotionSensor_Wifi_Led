LED strip controlled by dusk and motion sensor, as well as controlled by a device connected to the Wifi network (phone, tablet, computer).

The strip has 17 colored LEDs and 2 white LEDs. Colorful LEDs are divided into 7 separately controlled sections. White LEDs are another section. Colorful and white LEDs have automatic mode switched on separately. Each section as well as automatic modes can be controlled by PWM signals.
